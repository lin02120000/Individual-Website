The beautiful City of Charlottetown is located on the south shore of Prince Edward Island, just off the eastern shore of Canada, in the Gulf of St. Lawrence, separated from the mainland by the Northumberland Strait. Charlottetown is the capital city of Prince Edward Island, and is Canada’s “Birthplace of Confederation”. Designated by Canadian Heritage as a Cultural Capital of Canada, Charlottetown truly is a centre of culture.

The best golf in Canada resides in PEI, the finest white sand beaches, and the warmest waters north of Florida.
